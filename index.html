FAQs

What does a public speaking coach do?

I help individuals develop tools to manage nerves and improve their communication skills, confidence and presentation skills.

How can you help me?

First, I identify your strengths and challenges. I then create a customised plan and track your progress throughout our sessions, providing feedback and support.

What services and coaching formats do you offer?

I offer focused individual coaching (face to face or online) and group workshops. Please see the services page for more information.

What's the coaching process like?

After you book an initial consultation and I understand your needs, I'll create a structured plan which includes a recommended number of sessions.

How long are coaching sessions?

Typically thirty to sixty minutes, depending on your needs.

How much does coaching cost?

It is R800 for a one-hour session. Please see the booking/contact page for more information.

Do you offer payment plans?

We can discuss flexible payment options.

How will I know if coaching is working?

Based on the initial assessment, we'll set measurable goals and track your progress.

Can you guarantee results?

Specific outcomes aren't guaranteed. My approach has helped many achieve and even exceed their goals.
