Communication Skills Coaching

Make Dialogue Productive

Work consumes most of our days. Our ability to communicate with clarity, connection, purpose and empathy within the workspace largely determines our success and sense of well-being.

Whether we engage one-on-one or in a team, or whether we lead organizations, effective communication, paired with a deep capacity to listen, enhances important relationships.

Understanding your unique way of communicating and your colleagues' own styles keeps dialogue open. It could be your superpower in moments of pressure and conflict.

Grow your communication skills and tools:

  • Understand your unique communication style
  • Gain perspective on others' expressive styles
  • Work at mastering your typical triggers
  • Learn techniques for handling areas outside of your comfort zone
  • Develop the art of true listening and leadership skills

Voice and Speech Coaching

Speak with Natural Authority

Your voice and speech are powerful social tools; building blocks for effective communication. When you first meet someone, they form an impression based on what they hear.

Your voice can create clarity, convey vital information, and create connections. Yet it can also hold tension in the body, resistance, fear and judgment.

The breath is the engine that makes your free voice soar. When you are breathing optimally, your voice resounds with ease and your words land with more clarity and connection.

In coaching, I rarely speak to the voice and speech in isolation. I always will connect it to the breath and what is happening below the surface for you so that we can build a better whole.

Together, we'll grow your authentic voice:

  • Increase your connection to your natural speaking voice
  • Develop an open, resonant and free voice with breath support
  • Improve your speech clarity and articulation
  • Develop a natural vocal connection to listeners
  • Grow your capacity for finding natural rhythm and pace

Public Speaking and Presentation Skills

Harness Nerves to Give You the Edge

Whether you are pitching a product, communicating statistics or inspiring a team, public speaking and presentation skills matter. Clarity and courage foster engagement and action but nerves can get in the way.

Presenting is often challenging, especially if you are new to it. Time, financial and organizational pressures easily affect a presentation's performance.

Tried and tested strategies will enhance your public speaking skills so that you are heard and have real influence.

Individual or group presentation skills training will help you:

  • Manage your nerves and stay succinct
  • Fully connect with your listener
  • Be open to questions and challenges
  • Work with presentation aids without losing connection
  • Use body language, gesture and movement effectively
